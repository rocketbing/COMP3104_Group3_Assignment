Learning Devops is very important since the DevOps model is an organization's answer to increasing operational efficiency, accelerating delivery, and innovating products

The Five Primary Goals of DevOps
- Enhancing the frequency and pace with which software is released
- Improving the quality and dependability of software products
- Cutting the costs and hazards of software development
- Increasing client satisfaction and loyalty
- Achieving a market competitive advantage

The benefits of Devops
1. Improved collaboration between development and operational teams
2. More frequent and faster software releases
3. improved product quality and dependability
4. Cost savings and resource optimization
5. DevOps Best Practices
6. (CI) Continuous Integration
